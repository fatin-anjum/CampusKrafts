BRACU Admission Structure

The BRAC University admission exam is divided into 6 distinct sections. Our courses map perfectly to these requirements based on your desired major.

Exam Sections

  • Section A: English Language (General Grammar & Reading)
  • Section B: English Composition (Essay & Writing skills)
  • Section C: Mathematics (General Arithmetic & Algebra)
  • Section D: Higher Mathematics & Physics (For STEM/CSE majors)
  • Section E: Biology & Chemistry (For Pharmacy/Biosciences)
  • Section F: Drawing (For Architecture majors)
